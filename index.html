The journey

From a college desk in Kerala
to production code in Dubai.

Every developer has an origin story. This one is built on curiosity, a willingness to keep learning, and the belief that there's always something more interesting just around the corner.

2016 – 2019

The beginning.
BSc Physics, Kerala.

MES College, Ponnani. Three years of physics, formulas, and labs — and a quiet pull toward computers that wouldn't let go. While classmates looked at academia, something about software kept pulling harder. The idea of building things from nothing.

MES College · Kerala · BSc Physics
2019 – 2022

The pivot.
MCA, Calicut University.

Pursuing a Master of Computer Applications was the real turning point. Data structures. Web development. Databases. System design. Final year: a full-stack ERP system built from scratch. Watching it actually deploy and work — that feeling stuck.

Calicut University · MCA · 2022
Early 2023

The grind.
Interviews. Rejections. Growth.

The first job hunt was humbling. Technical rounds, whiteboard sessions, rejection emails. Early roles at Poonchola Technologies and Exthgen gave what no classroom does: owning code in production, shipping under pressure. Each setback just made the next round clearer.

India · First Roles · 2023
Mid 2023

The leap.
One-way ticket to Dubai.

July 2023. A flight. Everything packed in two bags. Dubai opened a different chapter — new city, different pace, bigger stage. AAAGENCY DMCC first, building hospital portals. Three months of learning to deliver fast and client-ready.

Dubai, UAE · July 2023
Oct 2023 – Now

The build.
Full-stack, full speed.

FACTS Computer Software House — contributing to FactsERP platform and shipping standalone products. Bakkah (automotive). TeamsTime (Flutter, both app stores). Tatmeen pharma scanner. Accounting software. E-commerce platform. Every project: a new domain, a new challenge. And now — AI is the next chapter.
